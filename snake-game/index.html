It's a Classic

From the classic arcade game to the nokia phone snake has been around for a while. The goal to eat apples and avoid obstacles. With such a simple goal there are variants which have explored single and multiplayer gameplay. The longer the game goes on the more likely it is that the snake will collide with it's own body causing the game to start again.

Game Play - WASD

The rules are simple. They are what they have always been and always will be. They are timeless and shall surpass the survival of the human race.

  • 1) You start with 3 lives.
  • 2) Steer the snake using the WASD keys to eat the green apple.
  • 3) If you hit a red obstacle or hit the body of the snake you loose a life.
