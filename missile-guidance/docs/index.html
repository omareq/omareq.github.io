missile-guidance

A program looking into the various methods of missile targeting. I have started with pursuit homing and have moved on to exploring proportional navigation. Further studies will include true proportional navigation, augmented proportional navigation, motion camoflage and perhaps if I am feeling bold I shall venture into optimal control theory.

Check out the Demo. Check out the Docs. Created using p5.js
